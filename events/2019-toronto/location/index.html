devopsdays Toronto - Location

For its sixth year, DevOpsDays Toronto moving to Evergreen Brick Works in the heart Toronto’s Don Valley.

Evergreen Brick Works: 550 Bayview Ave, Toronto, ON M4W 3X8


Getting There

There are a number of ways to get to Evergreen Brick Works from downtown Toronto. You can visit their Getting Here page for details.

Planes, Trains and Automobiles

Toronto has two airports: Toronto Pearson International Airport and Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport. Check Expedia.ca for flight information.

Union Station is a main transit hub for Toronto. You can get there by Via Rail, GO Transit and TTC. From the Pearson Airport the Up Express train will get you there in a short 20 minute ride.

Accommodations

Toronto is a world class city with a multitude of accomodation options in the downtown core. All are an easy Uber or Lyft ride to the venue.

We’ve arranged a room block at The Broadview Hotel.

Online reservations

  1. Visit www.thebroadviewhotel.ca
  2. Click on “book a room.”
  3. Click on “add code”
  4. Corporate code: 30086
  5. Choose your stay dates and your rate will show

Telephone reservations

  1. Toll-free number - (844) 362-8439
  2. Mention that is for an upcoming group reservation and the code is: 30086
  3. The agent will be able to provide you with a discounted rate.

Last day to reserve rooms is April 28, 2019.
